A Letter to Mom

Dear Mom,

There are no words powerful enough to capture everything you mean to us. You have been our first teacher, our safe harbour, our loudest cheerleader, and our softest place to land — all at once, all without complaint.

You woke up before dawn so we could sleep a little longer. You went without so we could have more. You smiled through the hard days and celebrated every small victory as if it were the greatest achievement in the world.

Today, we want you to know: it did not go unnoticed. Every sacrifice, every hug, every late-night conversation — we carry all of it, and it made us who we are.

Thank you for being exactly, perfectly, irreplaceably you.

With all our love, always ❤️
